About Our Models

Astrava models are quantitative frameworks that transform real-time market, macroeconomic, liquidity, mortgage, and on-chain data into actionable intelligence.

MRPI
Measures mortgage rate pressure through Treasury yields, volatility, macroeconomic conditions, and market structure.
LTPI
Measures global liquidity conditions across central banks, credit markets, and financial system liquidity.
MTPI
Measures macroeconomic trend strength using inflation, labor, growth, and financial conditions.
ITPI
Integrates macro, liquidity, mortgage, and digital asset data into a unified quantitative framework.
